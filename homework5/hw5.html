On the left is the vector design I created, and on the right is my work, "Indifference to Destruction", with the vector design implemented. The climate crisis symbol I created is an extention of the extinction symbol attributed to the East London artist, Goldfrog ESP. I used triangles and circles as recurring shapes for the extinction symbol design but transforming it into an hourglass. I aimed to use this symbol to demonstrate the limited time we have before the conditions affecting the Earth are irreversible.
