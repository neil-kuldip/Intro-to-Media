Focus in Light

Experimenting with the DSLR, I wanted to try taking a photo of something that was the central focus of the setting. However, I wanted that focus to shape the perspective of the adjacent area, which led me to taking the photo of this lit candle. After taking this photo it dawned on me how the candle symbolizes oneâ€™s own first-person perspective on the world and how our own vision shapes the way we view our own environment like that of the candle. At the same time, the duration of time this candle can stay lit is finite, just like ourselves and how we spend our time to illuminate pathways that are the most important to us. I used Photoshop to add adjustment level and saturation/hue layers to show more of the candle wick and lighting the background a bit more.
